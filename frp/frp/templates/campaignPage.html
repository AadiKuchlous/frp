Our Story

PK Foundation was established in 2000 to help bridge the gap in learning in schools through books and reading. PK Foundation is based out of Pune and has 1 more office in rural Maharashtra. With a staff of 12 members and 20 volunteers, PK foundations aims to establish libraries in Govt schools in rural Maharahstra.

Our work and it's impact

PK Foundation’s mission is to inculcate reading habit among students at a young age and to expose them to a large variety of books thus pushing their reading abilities and exposure to the works. Children, specially in rural Maharashtra don’t have access to any other books than curriculum and hence learning a language becomes a curriculum material again. We at PK Foundation, aim to introduce the joy of languages through story books to children and hence develop an interest in academics. Till date PK Foundation has established over 120 libraries in Govt schools in the districts of Akola and Paratwada. These libraries serve over 4800 childre from the age group of 7 to 14. Most children are first generation learners and their education is their family’s chance out of poverty

How will we utilize these funds.

PK Foundation aims to set up 400 new libraries by the end of year 2015.The collected funds will be utilized for buying books for children of all ages in 2 languages and buying cupboards for setting them up.
We aim to also start a book-lending program with a membership fee of Rs.1/- to remote areas. These funds would help the logistics for this program.
