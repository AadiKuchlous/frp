donate-a-book initative

This is the place for you to learn more about this effort, as well as what else we do at Pratham Books, in our mission to place a book in every child’s hand.

Pratham Books was founded with the ambition of putting a book in every child’s hand. To this end, we’ve spent a decade creating exciting, accessible stories for children, in languages they can relate to, and building a system that reaches them, all across the length and breadth of India. Along the way, we came to realise that there are thousands of people who would love to help children read, but are unaware of how they can help.

This led to the idea of a crowd-funding initiative – this initiative! – that would make it easy to help India’s children read. All a donor would have to do is come to the site, pick an NGO, librarian, or a Reading Champion they would like to support, and a few clicks would have them ordering a batch of books for a group of children to discover, read, and enjoy!

Our sole aim is to see more Indian children read, and that is why beneficiaries don’t pay us a rupee to receive their books. Every rupee donated on this platform goes towards the books, and ensuring they reach the children for whom they are meant.

Pratham Books

At Pratham Books, we believe that every child has the right to enjoy good books.  And we believe they should have stories set in surroundings familiar to them and in a language close to their culture. We believe that to make books accessible to every child, the price points have to be very low. Pratham Books is a brand of story books as Indian as the children who read them. As a not-for-profit publisher, our dream is to see a country where every child wants to read, is able to read, and has something good to read.

With a range of stories created to excite children with different likes and tastes, the books are illustrated by some of the most enthusiastic and talented illustrators. Our aim is to have something good for every child, in every part of the country, and in the process spread the joy of reading far and wide.

Till date, Pratham Books has published over 280 titles in English and 10 other Indian languages. That's over 1800 unique books, most of which are priced below Rs 35. We have printed over 12 million books, over 11 million story cards and have a readership of nearly 52 million. Our vision is to reach 200 million children in India, and we hope to someday put 'a book in every child's hand'.

FAQs

Beneficiary FAQs

1. Am I eligible to raise money for books?

If you are an individual or an organisation that works with children in India, and seek help to spread the joy of reading to these children, then you are eligible! You do not necessarily have to be a registered charity to build a campaign.

2. How long does it take to set up?

You will have to fill our 4 step form which will help us identify you and your work. the campaign is created, it will take us 2 to 3 working days to verify it. Once verified, your campaign goes live, and you can start spreading the word about your appeal.

3. How much does it cost?

There is no fees for setting up a Campaign. You can set up your Campaign for free and get donations for the books you require.

4. How long does a Campaign run for?

The average duration of a Campaign is 25 days but if you need more time, you can extend your campaign for a period of extra 5 days

5. What if I don’t achieve my set target?

Even if you don’t achieve 100% of your target, you are eligible to get books for whatever amount you have already raised. For example, if your target amount for Rs.10,000/- but at the end of 25/30 days you could raise only Rs.7500/-, you can still choose books for that amount. Books worth Rs.7500/- would reach you.

6. In how many days will I get the books?

After your campaign is over, a list of in-stock books is sent to you. You are required to revert within 10 working days with your choice and preference of books required. As soon as we get your list, the books will reach you within 30 working days.

7. What are the other things I should know before I start?

Do look at our Terms of Use before you start. If there are still any more questions, do drop in an email at donateabook@prathambooks.org.

Donors FAQs

1. Who do I donate to? How do I choose a beneficiary for my donation?

We have a number of beneficiaries listed who are running their campaigns from different parts of the country. You can choose a beneficiary whom you wish to donate to basis the geography : state or urband vs rural, or their total requirement or the scope of their work (Govt organisation vs Individual raisers). You can filter your choices also basis the language they are seeking books for. Use our ‘Easy Browse’ filter to narrow down your choices. You could also lend a hand to organisations who are least funded or whose campaigns are going to end soon.

2. Is it safe to donate online? Are my bank details safe?

www.donateabook.org.in has a SSL certification which means all your transactional information is safe with us. For our payment gateway we have partnered with a top Payment gateway service provider which has the highest level of security in place.

3. How can I be sure my donation is reaching the correct organisation?

As soon as the campaign ends, the process of delivery of books to the beneficiary will begin from our end. In less than a month, the books should be with the children. We encourage the beneficiary to put up a report on usage on the page which you can go through. You can also contact the beneficiary directly for any update. Pratham Books has been working in the space of children book publishing for over 10 years and is a not-for-profit with huge credibility in the sector.

4. Can I choose the books that I want to donate?

No. The option of choosing the books lies only with the Beneficiary as they know their children and their needs the best. Basis their requirement they choose a mix of books in various languages and levels.

5. Would the beneficiary know I am donating for them?

Yes, if you want them to. We have an option of donating anonymously which you can tick if you would prefer to keep your donation anonymous. Else, your name and message (if any) will be captured and will reflect on the beneficiary page.

6. Will I get anything in return?

The joy of knowing that you have helped more children in India is the biggest reward that your contribution will get you. There are no physical rewards if that’s what you meant.

7. When does my money reach the beneficiary?

The books that are brought from your donation reaches the Beneficiary within 30 days from when the campaign ends.

8. Can I change my chosen beneficiary or cancel my donation?

Once you have made the donation and paid via your bank a/c, no change or cancellation is possible.

9. Can I make a donation on someone else’s behalf?

Yes, very much. Donating for a cause is the best gift to give someone. You can choose to donate on their behalf by mentioning their name as the Donor while making the payment.
