Duration of Campaign

A campaign can raise funds for up to 60 days, but the average fully-funded campaign is only 47 days long. You campaign will run for the number of days that you select and will start from the day the campaign goes live. Please note that there will be a 30-45 day lead time after campaign ends and before you get your books. Therefore plan your campaign time wisely!

Multi-center funding

Are you raising funds for multiple centres? If yes please list the city and town names. Also note that shipping for all the books will be to one central location. If you have any special instructions for packaging please mention them here. e.g. If you are raising books in tamil for chennai centre and telugu for Hyderabad centre then you can add a note – Please pack the tamil and chennai books separately.
