About Pratham Books

Pratham Books is a not-for-profit profit publisher that publishes high quality books for children at affordable prices and in multiple Indian languages. With a range of stories created to excite children with different likes and tastes, the books are illustrated by some of the most enthusiastic and talented illustrators. Our aim is to have something good for every child, in every part of the country, and in the process spread the joy of reading far and wide. (more)

Success Stories

E Base Library

The E-Base library is a humble attempt to open up the world of books to the students of Pench. The importance of reading for a holistic education cannot be discounted and the E-Base library strives to fill this gap that exists by bringing to the students books from all around the world. This is the first time the students of this region have a library at their doorstep. Would you want to start a Library too?

Become a Reading Champion

Engage with us to spread the joy of storytelling by becoming a Pratham Books' Reading Champion. Through this champions program we encourage our community of volunteers to conduct reading sessions under the ‘One Day, One Story’ event that we do to celebrate the International Literacy Day on September 6th. These sessions are conducted free of cost and mostly with children from under-served communities. The Pratham Books' Champions program is a one-of its kind volunteer program that has scaled to a national level with more and more volunteers wanting to join us in this movement. Want to be a Reading Champion?
Write in to us at champions@prathambooks.org
