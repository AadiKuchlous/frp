What are you raising money for?

Please select what you wish to raise money for. For your convenience we have four types of donations to choose from.

  1. If you already have a functional library we recommend you choose just Books.
  2. If you wish to build a classroom library or need a modular unit with books try our 'Library-in-a-classroom or Books-in-a-bag. Click here to see how functional a little bag can be!
  3. If you are a school or an NGO that would like to raise money to give away books as prizes or distribute books choose the option titles Prizes.
  4. If you are a Reading champion who wants to raise books to promote the habit of reading choose the last option titled Reading Sessions.

Duration of Campaign

A campaign can raise funds for up to 30 days, but the average fully-funded campaign is only 25 days long. You campaign will run for the number of days that you select and will start from the day the campaign goes live. Please note that there will be a 5 days lead time after campaign ends and before you get your books. Therefore plan your campaign time wisely!
