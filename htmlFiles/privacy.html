What we collect!

We always want our policies to be clear and easy to understand — so this Privacy Policy offers a simple breakdown of what happens with your data on Donate-A-Book including which details are public, which are private, and which are shared only with Campaign Owners / Beneficiary Organizations whose projects you're supporting.

In order for you to create an account on Donate-A-Book and use the Service, we need to collect and process some information. Depending on your use of the Service, that may include:

  1. Information (such as your name, email and postal addresses, telephone number,website links etc) that you provide by completing forms on the Site, including if you register as a user of the Service, upload or submit any material through the Site, or request any information;
  2. Your login and password details, in connection with the account sign-in process;
  3. Details of any requests or transactions you make through the Service;
  4. Information about your activity on and interaction with the Site and Services, such as your IP address and the device or browser you use;
  5. Communications you send to us (for example, when you ask for support, send us questions or comments, or report a problem);

What’s Private

This data will not be publicly displayed or revealed to other users:

  1. Any payment information you provide
  2. Your password details
  3. Your IP address
  4. Your phone number
  5. Communications you send to us (for example, when you ask for support, send us questions or comments, or report a problem)

We don’t give your personal information to any third-party services, except when it’s necessary to provide Donate-A-Book Service (like when we partner with payment gateways to process donations).

We do reserve the right to disclose personal information when we believe that doing so is reasonably necessary to comply with the law or law enforcement, to prevent fraud or abuse, or to protect Donate-A-Book ‘s legal rights.

What’s Only Shared With

When you back a project, the Campaign Owners / Beneficiary Organizations will know your account name and the amount you have pledged. Campaign Owners / Beneficiary Organizations never receive Donors’ credit card details or any other payment information.

What's Public

When you create an account, we create a basic profile page for you on Donate-A-Book, containing your account name, the date the account was created, and a list of Campaigns you are running or supporting. Here are some of the things that will be publicly viewable on your profile, or elsewhere on the site:

  1. The account name, and the date it was created.
  2. Any information you choose to add to your profile (like a picture, bio, or your location).
  3. Campaigns that you are running.
  4. Campaigns that you are supporting or have donated to.
  5. Any comments you may have made on the Campaign pages.

Uses of Your Personal Information

We will use the personal information you provide to:

  1. Identify you when you sign in to your account;
  2. Enable us to provide you with the Services;
  3. Send you information we think you may find useful or which you have requested from us;
  4. Present projects to you when you use the Service which we believe will be of interest based on your geographic location and previous use of the Service;
  5. Administer your account with us;
  6. Enable us to contact you regarding any question you make through the Service;
  7. Analyze the use of the Service and the people visiting to improve our content and Services; and
  8. Use for other purposes that we may disclose to you when we request your information.

We take securing your data and preserving your privacy very seriously. We never post anything to your Facebook, Twitter, or other third-party accounts without your permission.

Email

  1. We want to communicate with you only if you want to hear from us. We try to keep emails to a minimum and give you the ability to opt out when we can.
  2. We will send you email relating to your personal transactions. You will also receive certain email notifications, from which you may opt out.
  3. We may send you service-related announcements on the rare occasions when it is necessary to do so.

Technology

Cookies are small pieces of information which are issued to your computer when you visit a website and which store and sometimes track information about your use of the Service. Donate-A-Book uses cookies to help recognize you as a repeat visitor, to improve the quality of our Service, and to try and make your browsing experience meaningful. When you enter our Site, our web server sends a cookie to your computer which allows us to recognize your computer but not specifically who is using it. By associating the identification numbers in the cookies with other customer information when, for example, you log-in to the Service, we know that the cookie information relates to you. Some of the code and cookies used by our Service are served by us, and some are served by third parties who are delivering services on our behalf.

Most web browsers automatically accept cookies but, if you prefer, you can change your browser to prevent that or to notify you each time a cookie is set. By blocking or deleting cookies used on our Service, you may not be able to take full advantage of our Service.

Wrap-up

To modify or delete the personal information you have provided to us, please log in and update your profile. We may retain certain information as required by law or for necessary business purposes.

Questions

If you have questions or suggestions, please email at donateabook@prathambooks.org
